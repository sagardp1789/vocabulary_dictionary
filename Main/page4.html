Hard word in sentences with it's meaning.

1.the uprising was suppressed with Abominable cruelty.

meaning:-

Abominable:  घृणास्पद :  loathsome, detestable, hateful

2.the rebel officers who led the Abortive coup were shot.

meaning:-

Abortive :  निष्पाप :   failed, unsuccessful

3.rumours of a further scandal abound.

meaning:-

abound:  विपुल:  be plentiful, be abundant

4."an abridge text of his speech.

meaning:-

abridge:  संक्षिप्त :   shorten, cut

5.a proposal to abrogate temporarily the right to strike.

meaning:-

abrogate :  रद्दीकरण :  repudiate, revoke
