Difficult words in sentences with meaning

1.After listening to the confusing lecture, the college girl was bemused.

meaning:-

bemused:मती गुंग झालेला:bewildered, confused

2.The fracture to my ankle would bench me for the entire season.

meaning:-

bench:खंडपीठ:pew, form

3.Today the Pope will make a benediction for all those who are sick and hungry.

meaning:-

benediction:आशीर्वाद:blessing, prayer

4.The benefaction from an anonymous donor eased the orphanage’s financial worries.

meaning:-

benefaction:फायदे:contribution, beneficence

5.My aunt received an award for being a benefactor to the public school.

meaning:-

benefactor:फायदे: patron, benefactress
