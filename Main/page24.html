Difficult words in sentences with meaning

1.You are supposed to exercise regularly for the behoof of your body, ensuring that is stays in top shape and capable.

meaning:-

behoof:अलभ्य लाभ:benefit, profit

2.It would behoove the president to listen to his advisers before making such an important decision.

meaning:-

behoove:आवश्यक असणे : be incumbent on

3.If you’re just going to continue to belabor the point, I’m going home.

meaning:-

belabor:बेलबोर: beat, hit, strike

4.The president’s belated decision came too late to save the hostages

meaning:-

belated:बेलेट:late, overdue

5.The rock climber started to belay one end of the rope to a sturdy rock and tied the other end around his waist.

meaning:-

belay :खाली:arrest, cease
